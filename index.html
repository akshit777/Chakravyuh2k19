What is Chakravyuh ?

Chakrvyuh: ease to enter, barriers to exit Inheriting the name from the roots of Indian history it symbolises indomitable fortitude, uncanny wit and hunger for success. So, fire up your soul and tighten your seatbelts, these power packed 7 days will ignite your soul and fasten your heartbeat because something riveting is coming for you.
